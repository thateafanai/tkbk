Privacy Policy for Apatani Biisi Kheta

Effective Date: Date - April 15, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how the "Apatani Biisi Kheta" application ("App"), published by the Apatani Baptist Association, Ziro ("Publisher", "we", "us", or "our"), handles information when you use the App.

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

This App is designed to function primarily offline and respects your privacy. We collect very limited information:

  1. Information Stored Locally on Your Device: User Preferences (Theme, Font Size, Font Family, Keep Screen Awake) and Favorite Song numbers are stored locally using shared preferences. This data remains entirely on your device. We do not collect or transmit this data.

  2. Information Handled by Third-Party Services (When You Initiate Action):

    • WhatsApp: Launching WhatsApp via the Feedback screen is subject to WhatsApp's own Privacy Policy.
    • Sharing: Using the Share button invokes the system share functionality; sharing is subject to the policies of the app you choose to share with.

  3. Technical Information (Potentially Collected): Basic, anonymous crash reporting or performance data might be collected by standard platform tools (Flutter, Google Play) solely for app improvement and bug fixing. This data does not personally identify you.

How We Use Information

Locally stored data is used only for App functionality and customization on your device. Anonymous technical data helps improve stability.

Data Storage and Security

Preferences and favorites are stored only locally on your device. You can clear this data via your device settings.

Data Sharing

We do not share your locally stored data. Interactions with third-party apps are governed by their policies. Anonymous technical data may be processed by standard platform tools.

Children's Privacy

This App is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy
